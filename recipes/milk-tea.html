Milk Tea

An intregal part of Nepali's day.

Milk Tea is like fuel to the start of the day and a detoxifier in the evening.

Steps to make Milk Tea.

  1. Pour 2 cups of milk on a cooking pot.
  2. Turn the stove on and set it at medium heat.
  3. Put a tbsp of sugar and 1 tbsp of Nepali tea leaves.
  4. Let it boil. Once it starts to boil, lower the heat and swirl with strainer, after the mixture settles, turn the heat back to medium.
  5. Repeat the process for 2 cycles.
  6. Turn off the stove and use the strainer to strain tea leaves and pour tea into a cup.
  7. Your tea is made. Enjoy
